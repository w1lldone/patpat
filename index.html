Pakanku

Farming Formulation Tips & Trick

Pakanku is an android-based mobile app that help farmers formulate feed for their livestock. Pakanku uses an industry-standard feed formula, so farmers can make the right feed for their purpose. In addition, there are tips and tricks for better farming, the latest price for feed ingredients, and feed usage history. Pakanku is the winner of Mobile Apps Development Competition on Vocomvest 2015.

Lookate

Travel Services Guides

Our very first project, Lookate is a travelers guide mobile app. Lookate gives you all information about tourist attraction, important place, culinary, and public services. Doesn't have a smartphone? No need to worry. Lookate uses Telkom's SMS API, so it can text you with all information you need. Lookate is the golden winner of BestApp ID 2014.
